EDUCATION
Raul first graduated from The National School of Bellas Artes Peru in 1990. In 2012 he took Print Media at Algonquin College.

EXHIBITIONS
Raul Also participated in many exhibitions and continues to showcase his artwork in Peru.
He won first price in sculpture in The National School of Bellas Artes, and has a second honourable mention at the II Biennal of Bellas Artes.
